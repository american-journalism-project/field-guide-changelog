# SeeGov

## Details
- URL: seegov.org
- Highlight: The See Gov platform helps civic creators and journalists produce and share video highlights of local government meetings.
- Summary: SeeGov is primarily a direct-to-public, creator platform for making video highlights of local government meetings. They provide a version of their platform specifically designed to assist newsrooms with local meetings coverage.

## What is this for?
SeeGov's primary output for each public meeting is a human-curated, video highlight package that summarizes and captures each meeting's most newsworthy moments. The highlights are broken up into clips that are optimized for sharing, embedding, and downloading. 

By focusing on creating easy-to-watch video highlights, SeeGov aims to “expand democratic participation” because it can “foster better understanding, create lasting memories of those involved in local government and their perspectives, and increase the likelihood of thoughtful participation.

### How is AI used?
SeeGov operates a network of Civic Creators, typically local members of their community who volunteer time to create video highlights for SeeGov, that do the work of clipping and creating the video highlight reels that are published on SeeGov.org. AI is used heavily in the extraction, summarization, and clipping process but ultimately the final output is human. 

With the use of AI, a typical four hour meeting report can be completed and published to SeeGov.org in under two hours or in some cases in only one hour.

### What makes it different from other tools?
SeeGov is a free to use, non-profit organization that is primarily focused on publishing human-generated video clips of as many public meetings as it can. Its goal is to expand participation in democracy not only by publishing clips that raise public awareness of specific events but also by engaging more citizens to become Civic Creators to cover their local community meetings.

Each public meeting a Civic Creator reports on is clipped into a series of human-edited video highlights. AI is used to speed up the process for creating each highlight, but the uniqueness of SeeGov's offering is equally in their ability to manage a network of Civic Creators and the technology those volunteers use to create highlight reels of public meetings.

### Pros
- Video highlights include timestamped summaries for easy navigation
- Creator platform can be customized for use by newsrooms

### Cons
- As a public-facing product, SeeGov’s output will scale as well as their Civic Creator network can scale.
