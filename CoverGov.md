# CoverGov 

## Details
- URL: covergov.com
- Highlight: CoverGov turns government data into searchable insights.
- Summary: CoverGov provides searchable transcripts, meeting overviews, and key data points of local government meetings

## What is this for?
CoverGov was initially built for government affairs professionals, but its functionality is broad enough to apply to a wider user base, including journalists and government officials. It can both summarize what happened in a meeting and provide a real-time transcript while a meeting is in progress.

The core functionality of CoverGov is a geographically-fed search engine that contains data from local government meetings (e.g., transcripts and official documents). This feature allows users to generate topic or keyword search queries across a wide or narrow geographical area of historical civic meeting data. Users can also save their complex search parameters for easy, regular access.

### How is AI used?
Meetings are processed nightly to merge and summarize multiple sources (transcript, agenda, related documents) to generate a full report.

Each meeting also offers a “chat” feature that allows for natural language conversations between the user and the transcript. The chat is limited to just the meeting and is not pulling in external sources that would lead to inaccuracies.

### What makes it different from other tools?
CoverGov’s real-time, live meeting transcriptions are built with 15 second delay. Its report generator tool can generate a handy PDF summary of individual meetings. The natural language “chat” option is also a feature not seen in other tools yet. 

### Pros
- Offers searchable transcripts that combine transcripts, agendas, official meeting documents, and other supporting materials

### Cons
- No free version available
