# SatchelAI

## Details
- URL: contentedai.com
- Highlight: A collection of AI-powered creation tools and public meeting transcription service.
- Summary: Satchel (now operating inside ContentedAI) offers a suite of AI-backed tools for newsrooms and journalists that intend to speed up the story creation process and allow editors and reporters to cover meetings more efficiently. Its intended use case is to create AI-written story drafts that should be reviewed and edited by a human before publishing.

## What is this for?
Satchel begins with a file upload. Users need to start with a recording or transcript and upload it into the interface. From there, users can select the various outputs they want to be generated from each recording upload. For example, the following can be generated from a single recording file:
- Meeting Summary - Create a detailed and concise summary
- Action Table - Track tasks, due dates, and can be downloaded as CSV
- Deep Dive - Converts a topic into a summary (use with Additional Instructions)
- Decision Log - Summarise key decisions made and the rationale behind them
- SWOT Analysis - Identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats
- Risk Matrix - Evaluate and prioritise potential risks 
- Key Metrics - Quickly surface and track metrics discussed
- Q&As - Automatically organise questions and answers from interactions for easy reference
- Musing (One Speaker) - Turn your rambling thoughts into a structured summary
- References - A helpful reminder of references and recommendations

### How is AI used?
Satchel’s mission aims to bridge “the gap between advanced AI and critical human elements, empowering journalists to do their very best work, efficiently.” During the creation process, you can instruct the AI to follow a specific “news angle” and use additional prompts to move the story creation in the direction you want.

The co-founders are from Microsoft, so it’s reasonable to expect that Satchel operates with those services. Its output is focused “on enhancing the role of journalists as well as the quality of their output.”

### What makes it different from other tools?
Satchel is a newsroom content generation tool that is powered entirely by generative AI. It is purposely intended for “‘news deserts’ - communities lacking in local journalism” and it "aims to revitalize local news coverage, allowing journalists to produce more meaningful stories in less time, ensuring communities stay informed and engaged."

### Pros
- Satchel works fast. You can go from having a recording file or transcript to a fully written story in just a few minutes.

### Cons
- There’s no automatic feed ingestion, so the tool is largely intended for first person interviews.
